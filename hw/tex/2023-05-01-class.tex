Show that the simplex gradient estimate can also be written as the
solution to the linear system
\[
  \begin{bmatrix}
    x_0 & 1 \\
    x_1 & 1 \\
    \vdots & \vdots \\
    x_n & 1
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix} \hat{\nabla} f \\ \lambda \end{bmatrix} =
  \begin{bmatrix} f(x_0) \\ \ldots \\ f(x_n) \end{bmatrix}.
\]
